 and this is what I've done so far:

  • I've work with Jose-Luis Rodriguez @ Universidad Complutense de Madrid on the gene expression effect in neuroblastoma cell cultures induced by allethrin, a pyrethroid.

  • I was the founder president of the Circle of Vetarinary Studies in Epidemiology @ UNMSM, a student group focused on epidemiology-related talks/research/training.
